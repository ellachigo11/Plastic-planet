How Plastic Recycling Works

1

Collection

You put plastic items in recycling bins. Trucks collect them from homes and businesses.

2

Sorting

Plastics are separated by type (PET, HDPE, etc.) using machines and workers.

3

Cleaning

Plastic is washed to remove labels, glue, dirt, and any food residue.

4

Shredding

Clean plastic is cut into small pieces called flakes or pellets.

5

Melting

Plastic pieces are melted down and formed into pellets for manufacturing.

6

New Products

Pellets are used to make new items like bottles, clothing, furniture, and more!
